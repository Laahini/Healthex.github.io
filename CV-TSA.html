Cumberland Valley TSA

TSA stands for technological student association and is a national organization that creates leaders in STEM. Every year, TSA holds a competition that consists of regional, state and national conferences where students can compete in team or individual events. The events range from a series of topics from architecture to coding. TSA is recognized worldwide and the organization ahs brances in almost every state. TSA works to create our future leaders in STEM not only in high school, but also elementary, middle school and in college.

Learn more about TSA on its official website: tsaweb.org

Our TSA Chapter team and advisors help run and improve the CV-TSA chapter. They act as role models, help plan fundraising events, and create a friendly atmosphere. Our chapter is competitive but, we still enjoy ourselves and have fun.

We vote our officers annually using the State TSA election guidelines. Our officers work hard to ensure that our chapter succeeds and has fun. This year, for example, they placed the burden of financing the State conference registration upon themselves.

We also have two middle school chapters from our school district, Mountain View TSA and Eagle View TSA. As a whole, the officers and advisors from both middle school and high school collaborate for the CV TSA as a whole.
