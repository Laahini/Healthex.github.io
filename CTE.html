CAREER AND TECHNICAL EDUCATION

Cumberland Valley high school has a large variety of career pathways available for students to chose or students can create their own pathway following a set of guidelines. There are pathways avialable in agriculture, art, business, Family and Consumer Sciences, Music, STEM, and more.

Technical education in Cumberland Valley is also offered at a vocational tech school where computer programming, construction, technology and logistics, machine technology and computer networking are taught. At Cumberland Valley high school, classes also include hands-on approach to learning and offer a wide variety from traditional classes to independent studies.
