What does telemedicine mean?

Telemendicine is also known as telehalth and digital health. It is basically a way that patients and doctors can use technology to communicate and safely carry out appointments. Telemedicine is a revolutionary industry that focuses on using technology to medical advantage.

What is telemedicine used for?

Telemedicine is used in sitautions such as the COVID 19 pandemic to salfely carry out appointments without exposure. It can also be used to reduce doctors fees and waiting time. Telemedicine is the new type of easy, affordable medical care.

Is breast cancer genetic?

Most women who are diagnosed with breast cancer do not have previous family history of breast cancer. Having a family member with breast cancer doesn't indicate that you will have breast cancer. However, it is known that some genes are associated with greater risk of breast cancer.

Can drinking water with lemon prevent kidney stones?

Drinking half-a-cup of lemon juice concentrate added with water over the course of a day can act as a preventive measure agaisnt calcium oxalate stone formation.

Is Greek yogurt healthier than regular yogurt?

Greek yogurt has more protein than regular yogurt, but that does not mean it's healthier. When choosing yogurt, fat-free or low-fat is the best option along with yogurt that has less sugar. Excess consumption of yogurt is also unhealthy.

How do I cure my depression?

Changing your lifestyle, spending more time with friends, exercising, enjoying a new hobby or passion and finding time for personal time can help overcome your depression. However, in the case of suicidal thoughts or severe depression, seek a professional health expert such as Healthex's therapists and counselors for help.

Is a Daily Glass of Wine Healthy?

Although small consumptions of alcohol, such as red wine, is beneficial in preventing heart disease and lowering risk for stroke and diabetes. Heavy drinking is obviously very injurous to health and can increase risk for liver and heart damage or cancer.

How long am I contagious when I have the flu or cold?

The contagious period for the flu can last 5 to 7 days from the first day of symptoms and sickness. The contagious period for a cold is 1 to 2 days before symptoms and can last up to 2 weeks under some circumstances.

Will staring at my computer 24/7 make me blind?

Staring at a computer screen cause eyestrain and fatigue but will not cause you to go blind. Your eyes may feel tired and cause eye pain or headaches. You can help relax your eyes by taking short naps, placing a cold spoon on your eyes for a few secods or making sure to blink often.

Are vaccines safe?

Vaccines are safe and effective. Every vaccine is tested and evaluated before being avialable to the public. They have recommended ages and vaccines work when giving them to healthy individuals to build up resistance. Unlike many false claims of unsafety, vaccines actually protect children from various diseases.
