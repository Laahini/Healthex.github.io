About Us

Our Founders

Two teenagers from Cumberland Valley High School decided to participate in a competition called TSA. Through this competition, they were inspired to start a medical telecommunications company that would be extremely beneficial during the COVID-19 pandemic, which had took a toll on themselves and millions of patients around the world. They realized how patients who normally received care immediately are not being able to meet with doctors due to unavailability and the risks for in-person appointments.

What We Stand For

Our founders started Healthex to give medical help to all of those who need it as quick as possible. We want to keep our patients and doctors safe, reduce waiting times and make the most out of an appointment.
