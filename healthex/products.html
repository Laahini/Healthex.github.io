What is a Plan of Action(POA)?

A Plan of Action is a unique and specific treatment plan for an individual who requires further appointments, check-ins and medicinal products for their medical condition. A Plan of Action gives patients a specialized doctor, specific to their needs, who will assess the condition, prescribe medicine and set up follow-up appointments to monitor or progress treatments.

A plan of action may last from 4 days to as long as necessary for a complete treatment. Plan of Actions are available for all types of cases, if they are needed, and costs accordingly to the supplies and appointments needed. The price range usually revolves around an additional $20 - $150 dollars to the cost for the initial appointment.
