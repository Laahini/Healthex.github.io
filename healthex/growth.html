Our Start

On December 14, 2020, our Healthex co-founders started the development of a new platform for easy, safe medical assistance during the COVID-19 pandemic.

Launch

Healthex launched on January 25, 2021 to start its journey as a rising medical telecommunication service.

Healthex's upcoming projects focus on adding more services to our platform, such as dermatology and optomerolgy. We plan on increasing our team of doctors and health professionals, allowing for faster and more appointments. New online applications will be released to focus on common needs and health wellness.
