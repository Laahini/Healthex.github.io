Our Mission

To connect experienced doctors to patients safely and efficiently

Healthex hopes to help guide patients and help them feel better all through online treatment.

Healthex was started to provide an affordable, safe visit to the doctors, especially during the COVID-19 global pandemic. We pride ourselves on providing quality feedback and treatment, even doing so through our online products such as the Healthex COVID-19 screening app. Taking care of your health is a top prioroty but in conditions where it is tough to visit a doctor in person, Healthex is your best bet. Have you ever had to wait hours after your scheduled appointment to visit a doctor? We're punctual and always update you on EVERYTHING!
